Thermo-Snooker

Simulation(with animation) of gas molecules inside a two-dimensional container, designed as part of a second-year thermodynamics course. The simulation models molecular motion, collisions, and thermodynamic properties using object-oriented programming (OOP) principles in Python.

Planet Detection

Method used on confirming the existence of a star or a planet by using data from NASA to obtain their radius and other parameters to check if it is within the range of the classification of a star or a planet. This method is a based on the combination of solar oscillator from lightkurve package and ACF(autocorrelation Function).
